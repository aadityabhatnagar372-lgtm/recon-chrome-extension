# Privacy Policy for One-click Recon

Last updated: May 19, 2026

One-click Recon ("we", "our", or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension handles user data.

---

## 1. Information Gathering and Usage
One-click Recon is a passive reconnaissance and developer security tool. 
- **Active Tab Data:** The Extension utilizes the `activeTab` permission strictly to read the domain name (hostname) of the active website when you manually click the Extension icon. 
- **No Data Collection:** The Extension **does not collect, store, or transmit** any personally identifiable information (PII), browsing history, locations, or user credentials.
- **Local Processing:** All domain analyses are initiated by the user. The hostname is processed locally and queried against public APIs (such as crt.sh, Shodan, and Wayback Machine) and your local API server to fetch technical information. We do not run any remote tracking servers, database backups, or user analytics.

---

## 2. Data Transference to Third Parties
We do not sell, trade, or transfer any data processed by the Extension. The data (domain name) is strictly sent to the public endpoints explicitly queried during passive search (e.g. Internet CDX, certificate logs) to show domain results to you, the user.

---

## 3. Policy Compliance
The Extension complies with the Google Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies, including the User Data Policy's requirements for Limit Use.
